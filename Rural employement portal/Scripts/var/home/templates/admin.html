Welcome to the Rural Employment Portal

Empowering Rural India, One Opportunity at a Time

The Rural Employment Portal is your gateway to a brighter future. We're committed to providing rural households with sustainable employment opportunities, enhancing livelihood security, and fostering community development.

Our mission is to make a difference in the lives of those who depend on unskilled labor. Through the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act (MGNREGA), we offer at least 100 days of guaranteed wage employment every year, creating a brighter tomorrow for rural India.




Guaranteed Employment

Access at least 100 days of employment every year, creating stability for rural households.

Fair Wages

Receive wages at par with the minimum wage of the state, ensuring fair compensation.

Community Development

Participate in projects that improve rural infrastructure and benefit your local community.
